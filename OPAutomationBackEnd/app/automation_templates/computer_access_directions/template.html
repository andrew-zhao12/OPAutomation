University Resources

 

These are the resources you will use during your time at Northwest. They can be accessed from the Login screen (https://www.nwmissouri.edu/login/) using your Network Username and the password you set following the steps above.

 

  • CatPAWS:

CatPAWS is your university account providing you access to registration, billing, transcripts, financial aid and more.
First-time login video: https://use.vg/SIvqYv

 

  • Northwest Email:

Information disseminated from University offices will be sent to you through your University assigned e-mail account.  It is the student’s responsibility to regularly check their University e-mail to be assured that time-sensitive information is received and read.

 

  • Northwest Online (Canvas):

Is a campus-wide course management system for supplementing campus-based and blended courses. All information regarding your courses will be located in Northwest Online. This is where you will spend a majority of your time completing your courses:

 

  • Degree Audit:

With this tool, you can see what courses are required for your degree, how transfer coursework has been applied, and monitor your progress toward graduation.
